Travel to Puerto Rico

Food! Culture! Vacation!

Puerto Rico is a Caribbean island and unincorporated U.S. territory with a landscape of mountains, waterfalls and the El Yunque tropical rainforests

San Juan is the captial and the whole country has a population of 3.194 million (2019)

Cool things to do in Puerto Rico are:

  1. Snorkel
  2. Scuba Dive
  3. Tour the Bacardi Rum Factory

Some foods to eat are:

  1. Alcapurrias
  2. Arroz Con Gandules
  3. Empanadillas
